# Cost of Living Guide for Indian Students Abroad

## Overview

All figures are monthly averages for a student sharing accommodation (not living alone). Converted to INR at mid-2025 rates. Actual costs vary by city, lifestyle, and exchange rate.

---

## Money-Saving Tips for All Countries

- **Cook at home:** Can save ₹10,000–₹25,000/month vs eating out
- **Student discounts:** ISIC card, NUS Extra (UK), student Oyster/transit passes
- **Second-hand shops:** Charity shops (UK), Vinted, Kleinanzeigen (Germany), Facebook Marketplace
- **Indian grocery stores:** Every major city has them — dal, rice, masalas available
- **Part-time work:** Earn ₹30,000–₹80,000/month depending on country and hours allowed
- **University food banks:** Most universities offer emergency food support — use without shame
- **Subscription sharing:** Split Netflix/Spotify with flatmates
