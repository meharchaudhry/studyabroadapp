# Student residence permit for university or higher professional education 

_Last update: 21 October 2025_

You want a residence permit to study in the Netherlands at a university or institute of higher professional education (in Dutch: _hoger beroepsonderwijs or HBO_). Only this educational institution can apply for a residence permit for you. Find out what the requirements are and how your educational institution can apply for a student residence permit.
